Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? Check out the FAQs below.

What types of theatre services do you offer? ˅ We specialize in technical theatre services, including stage management, lighting design and operation, sound operation, and event coordination.
Can you provide support for non-theatre events? ˅ Yes! While theatre is our main focus, we also assist with technical and stage management needs for events such as school productions, festivals, and community projects.
How far in advance do I need to book your services? ˅ We recommend booking as early as possible to ensure availability, especially during peak seasons. For large-scale productions, a lead time of at least 4-6 weeks is ideal.
Do you work with schools and educational institutions? ˅ Absolutely! We have extensive experience working with schools on productions, nativities, arts festivals, and staff performances.
What equipment do you use? ˅ We are proficient with a range of industry-standard tools, including Granite Sound comms systems, advanced lighting desks, and audio mixing setups.
Can you adapt to specific technical requirements? ˅ Yes, we tailor our approach to meet your project's unique technical needs. Let us know your setup or requirements, and we’ll integrate seamlessly with your team.
What are your prices? ˅ Visit the Pricing page for price lists, or visit the Quote page for a quote.
Do you provide your own equipment? ˅ Our services primarily focus on operating and managing technical systems, but we can help coordinate equipment rentals if needed.
Are you able to work on short notice? ˅ We’ll do our best to accommodate last-minute requests, but availability cannot be guaranteed. Contact us as soon as possible to discuss your needs.
What is your cancellation policy? ˅ Cancellations made more than 7 days in advance are eligible for a full refund of any deposit paid. For cancellations within 7 days, refunds will depend on the circumstances and prior agreements.
How can I contact you for more information? ˅ You can reach us via email at me@benbradfordtheatre.run.place. We’ll be happy to discuss your project in more detail.
